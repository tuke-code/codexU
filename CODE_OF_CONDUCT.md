# Code of Conduct

codexU contributors are expected to keep discussions technical, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive when reporting problems or reviewing changes.
- Respect that usage data, local paths, screenshots, and Codex thread titles may contain private information.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on reproducible behavior, implementation tradeoffs, and maintainable fixes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private account data, local Codex databases, private thread content, or private local paths without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask to refocus.

## Reporting

For conduct or privacy concerns, contact the maintainers privately. Security-sensitive reports should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
